Course Policies

Attendance & Participation

Regular attendance and active participation are essential. Students are expected to complete the required readings before class and come prepared to contribute thoughtfully to discussion.

Late Work

Bi-weekly reports and other assignments are due at the specified time. Unless otherwise noted, a penalty of 20% per day will be applied for late submissions, up to a maximum of 5 days, after which the assignment will receive no credit.

Academic Integrity

All work submitted must be your own and must adhere to the University of Toronto’s policies on academic integrity. Plagiarism and other forms of academic misconduct will be handled according to university regulations.
